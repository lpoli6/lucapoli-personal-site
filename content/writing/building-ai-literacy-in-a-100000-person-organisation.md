Large organisations need different levels of AI fluency for executives, managers, operators, and technical builders, each tied to real decisions and workflows. This post will outline a staged capability model that links education to governance, tooling, and measurable behavior change. The central claim is that literacy only matters when it changes how teams prioritize work and execute with better judgment.
