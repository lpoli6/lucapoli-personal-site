Deep-tech teams succeed by combining long-horizon bets with ruthless short-cycle execution, and CRE increasingly needs the same operating rhythm. This piece will explore how portfolio decisions, building operations, and tenant experience can benefit from product-minded experimentation and clearer feedback loops. The angle is practical: what to copy, what to avoid, and how to adapt these patterns to a highly regulated asset-heavy sector.
