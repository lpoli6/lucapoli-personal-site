title: "PropTech's false starts, and what the next decade requires"

excerpt: PropTech has seen repeated hype cycles because many products optimized for fundraising narratives, not the operational reality of property workflows.

---

The sector has no shortage of ideas, but too many solutions fail once they meet fragmented data, legacy systems, and misaligned incentives across owners, operators, and occupiers. This draft will examine why earlier waves underdelivered and which capabilities are now becoming structurally viable, especially around AI-enabled operations and decision support. The angle is sober optimism: fewer moonshots, more durable compounding bets.
