---
title: "Why ontology design is the hardest part of enterprise AI"
date: 2026-02-22
excerpt: Ontology design is difficult because it forces organisations to reconcile messy reality with clean abstractions that must still run day to day.
draft: true
---

Model performance gets attention, but ontology decisions quietly lock in how teams understand and operate the business. This draft will argue that ontology work is primarily organisational design: naming, ownership, boundaries, and change management disguised as data architecture. The intended takeaway is a framework for building ontologies that stay useful under real operational pressure.
