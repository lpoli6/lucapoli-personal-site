Some enterprises need integrated platform-plus-operator execution because their data, workflows, and governance are deeply entangled; others can move faster with lighter, modular stacks. This article will define the conditions where the Palantir model compounds and where it introduces unnecessary cost or dependency. The goal is to give operators a decision lens grounded in context, not brand narratives.
