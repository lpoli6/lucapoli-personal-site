---
title: "The AI demo-to-deployment gap is where value is won or lost"
date: 2026-02-22
excerpt: Most AI initiatives fail not in model quality, but in the operational trench between impressive demo and accountable production.
draft: true
---

Enterprise teams rarely struggle to produce a compelling AI demo; they struggle to integrate that capability into workflows, controls, and incentives that survive quarter after quarter. This draft will map the predictable failure points between pilot and production, from unclear ownership to brittle data contracts and weak adoption loops. The core argument is that deployment discipline, not frontier model novelty, determines business outcomes.
